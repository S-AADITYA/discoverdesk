# DiscoverDesk — simple guide

This explains the app in plain words. No tech knowledge needed.

## 1. What this is
A shared workspace for your agency. Three teams use it:
- **Sales** and **KAM** — bring in clients and ask for influencer profiles.
- **Discovery** — find the influencers and send back a sheet.
- **Admin** — approves people and controls who can see what.

Everyone opens the *same* app, so everyone sees the same live data.

> **Local vs live team mode:** the app works instantly on your computer (local mode). To let your whole team log in from different computers and share one live database, follow **SETUP.md** — it's free and about 10 minutes, no coding. The pill at the top shows "Local only" or "Live · Cloud".

## 2. First time you open it (create your admin)
- The very first time, you'll see **Set up your workspace**.
- Type your name, work email, and a PIN (or password in cloud mode). Click **Create admin & enter**.
- That first account is **you, the permanent admin**. It can't be removed, so you're never locked out.
- Your team does **not** sign themselves up. You create their logins: **People & Access → Create account** → enter name/email/department → the app gives you a **PIN** to hand them. They sign in with email + PIN. You can view or reset anyone's PIN anytime.
- **You stay logged in.** The app never logs you out on its own. Your work is saved the second you do it.
- Prefer to look around first? On the setup screen click **Load demo data** to fill it with a sample team and data. Later, an admin can **Reset workspace** (in People & Access) to wipe it and start clean.

## 3. The main flow (start to finish)
1. **Enquiry** — Sales/KAM log a new client in **Enquiries**. When it's serious, they click **→ Request**.
2. **Request** — This opens a form. Fill in the brand, niche, follower range, how many profiles, deadline, notes. Click **Raise request**.
3. **Assign** — Admin (or the form) picks a Discovery person. That person gets a notification.
4. **Deliver** — The Discovery person opens the request, clicks **Upload profile sheet**, and picks their Excel/CSV file. The app reads it and sends it for review. This is **Round 1**.
5. **Review, one influencer at a time** — The Sales/KAM person opens the request and sees every influencer in a table with two buttons:
   - ✓ = keep this influencer
   - ✗ = reject this influencer (type a short reason in the Feedback box)
   Then they choose:
   - **Approve kept & deliver** — done, the good ones are delivered.
   - **Request round 2** — sends it back to Discovery with the exact list of rejected influencers and your feedback.
6. **Round 2, 3…** — Discovery uploads a new sheet with replacements. Same review again. Every round is kept and stacked, so you always see the full history.

## 4. Brand sheets (influencers per brand)
- Go to **Brand Sheets**.
- Each brand has its **own separate sheet** — click the brand's tab at the top.
- Add an influencer by hand, or **Import sheet** to bring in a whole Excel/CSV at once.
- Set each influencer's status: shortlisted, contacted, confirmed, rejected.
- **Download sheet** exports that brand's list to Excel.

## 5. Downloads (exports)
Look for the **Export / Download** buttons on Requests, Enquiries, Brand Sheets, each round, and Reports. They save a real Excel file to your computer. Rejected/kept decisions and feedback are included when you export a round.

## 6. Email
- The **Inbox** (bell icon, top right) shows every alert: assignments, submissions, approvals, rejections.
- Click **Email** on any alert to open a ready-to-send message in your normal mail app.
- **Email settings** lets each person choose which events email them.

## 7. Who can see / do what (permissions)
- Admin opens **People & Access**.
- The **Access plan** table shows what each department can do by default.
- Click **Permissions** on any person to turn abilities on/off just for them.
- The first Admin is **permanent** and can't be removed, so you never get locked out.

## 8. Never losing data
- Everything saves automatically to the shared workspace.
- In **People & Access**, click **Download full backup** any time to keep a copy of *everything* in one file.
- **Restore backup** puts it all back in one click if you ever need to.

## 9. Reports
The **Reports** page updates itself: total requests, delivered, rejection rate, average rounds, who's raising the most, turnaround per Discovery person, rejections by brand, the enquiry funnel, and your influencer roster by status.

## 10. Where this goes next
This version runs fully in the browser — great for your team to start using today. When you want it "properly hosted" (encrypted passwords, real email that sends by itself, and connecting other apps like your CRM or campaign tools), the screens stay the same and we plug them into a hosted backend. The data is already organised in clean pieces (people, brands, requests, rounds, influencers, enquiries, campaigns) so those future connections are straightforward.

Tip: keep a backup file once a week. That's your safety net.
